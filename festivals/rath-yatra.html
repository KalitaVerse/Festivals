Rath Yatra

Rath Yatra, also known as the Festival of Chariots, is a grand Hindu festival dedicated to Lord Jagannath. Celebrated in Puri, Odisha, the festival marks the annual journey of the deities on massive chariots, symbolizing unity, devotion, and divine grace.
